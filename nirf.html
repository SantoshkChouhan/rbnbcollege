National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF)

About NIRF

The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) was launched by the Ministry of Education, Government of India, in 2015. The framework outlines a methodology to rank institutions across the country based on specific parameters. The primary objective of NIRF is to create a performance matrix for educational institutions that would foster healthy competition among them, leading to overall improvement in the quality of education.

R. B. Narayanrao Borawake College participates in the NIRF ranking process annually, demonstrating our commitment to transparency, quality assurance, and continuous improvement.

NIRF Parameters

Teaching, Learning & Resources

Focuses on student strength, faculty-student ratio, faculty qualifications, financial resources and their utilization.

Weightage: 30%

Research & Professional Practice

Measures the quantity and quality of research output, professional practice, and collaborative activities.

Weightage: 30%

Graduation Outcomes

Evaluates the performance of students in terms of graduation rates, university ranks, and their progression to higher studies.

Weightage: 20%

Outreach & Inclusivity

Assesses the institution's efforts in inclusive education, including diversity in terms of geographical, gender, and socio-economic aspects.

Weightage: 10%

Perception

Measures the perception of the institution among academics, employers, and the general public based on surveys conducted by NIRF.

Weightage: 10%

NIRF Improvement Initiatives

R. B. Narayanrao Borawake College is dedicated to improving its performance in the NIRF rankings through various initiatives:

  • Strengthening faculty development programs to enhance teaching-learning processes
  • Promoting research activities and publications in high-impact journals
  • Improving industry-academia collaborations for better student placement and exposure
  • Enhancing infrastructure and learning resources
  • Implementing feedback mechanisms for continuous improvement
  • Focusing on inclusive education practices and diversity
  • Developing robust documentation and data management systems
