Principal's Message

Dear Students, Parents, and Visitors,

Welcome to R. B. Narayanrao Borawake College, Shrirampur. Since its establishment in 1960, our institution has been committed to providing quality education and shaping the future of students from all walks of life. As an autonomous college with A+ NAAC accreditation, we take immense pride in our rich legacy and continuous pursuit of excellence.

Our institution is deeply committed to the vision of our founder, the esteemed Karmaveer Bhaurao Patil, who believed that education is the most powerful tool for social transformation. In keeping with this vision, we strive to provide our students with not just academic knowledge but also moral values and practical skills to help them succeed in life and contribute meaningfully to society.

At RBNB College, we combine traditional teaching methodologies with modern technologies and innovative learning approaches. Our well-qualified faculty, state-of-the-art infrastructure, research facilities, and vibrant campus provide an ideal environment for holistic education and personal growth.

I encourage all students to make the most of the opportunities available at our institution and to actively participate in both academic and extracurricular activities. Remember, education is not just about earning a degree; it's about becoming a well-rounded individual who can face the challenges of the future with confidence and contribute to the betterment of society.

Together, let us continue the legacy of excellence and social commitment that has been the hallmark of RBNB College.

Sincerely,
Dr. Rajendra S. Jagdale
Principal
