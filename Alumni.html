Alumni Association

Welcome to RBNB Alumni Network

The RBNB College Alumni Association strives to build and maintain a lifelong connection between the college and its alumni. Through this network, we aim to create a platform for mutual support and growth, enabling our alumni to remain an integral part of the college community.

Our alumni are our greatest ambassadors, and we take immense pride in their achievements and contributions to society. This platform provides an opportunity for former students to reconnect with their alma mater, interact with current students, and network with fellow alumni.
