College Autonomy

Excellence Through Academic Autonomy

R. B. Narayanrao Borawake College was granted academic autonomy in 2018, empowering us to design and implement innovative curricula tailored to the needs of our students and society.

What is Autonomy?

Academic autonomy refers to the freedom granted to educational institutions to design their own curricula, implement teaching methods, conduct assessments, and award degrees, all within the broad framework provided by the affiliating university. This freedom allows institutions to be more responsive to the changing needs of students, industry, and society.

R. B. Narayanrao Borawake College was granted autonomy by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and Savitribai Phule Pune University in 2018, making it one of the select institutions in the region to enjoy this status. This recognition came as a result of the college's consistent academic excellence, robust infrastructure, qualified faculty, and commitment to quality education.

Benefits of Autonomy

Curriculum Design

Freedom to design and regularly update curricula that is relevant, contemporary and aligned with industry needs. We can incorporate emerging fields and technologies promptly without bureaucratic delays.

Innovative Teaching

Flexibility to implement innovative teaching methodologies, experiential learning approaches, and interdisciplinary studies that enhance the learning experience of students.

Assessment Systems

Authority to design and implement comprehensive evaluation systems that assess not just theoretical knowledge but also practical skills, critical thinking, and application abilities.

Industry Collaborations

Enhanced ability to forge meaningful collaborations with industry partners, leading to internships, projects, and employment opportunities for students.

Student Development

Focused approach to holistic student development through specialized courses, skill enhancement programs, and value-added certifications that improve employability.

Global Perspective

Ability to incorporate global educational trends and practices, preparing students to compete and excel in an increasingly internationalized job market.

Autonomy Journey Timeline

2016

Initial Application

College submitted the initial application for autonomy to UGC and Savitribai Phule Pune University after careful preparation and meeting all eligibility criteria.

2017

UGC Expert Committee Visit

UGC Expert Committee visited the college to assess infrastructure, faculty qualifications, academic achievements, and administrative capabilities.

May 2018

Autonomy Status Granted

UGC and Savitribai Phule Pune University granted autonomous status to the college for an initial period of 10 years, recognizing its academic excellence.

August 2018

Autonomous Governance Structure

Establishment of Academic Council, Board of Studies for each department, and other statutory bodies as per UGC guidelines.

2019

First Autonomous Batch

Implementation of the first set of autonomous curricula and admission of the first batch of students under the autonomous system.

2022

Curriculum Revision

Comprehensive revision of all autonomous curricula based on feedback from stakeholders and to align with NEP 2020 guidelines.
